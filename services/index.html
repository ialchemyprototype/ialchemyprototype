Innovative Problem Solving

From Softgoods to Hardgoods

Since 2003 we have innovated and excelled in new and existing problem spaces. Through a collaborative process, we imagine and take action to develop any project from fundamental building blocks to valuable products, services and systems. At Industrial Alchemy we co-design with clients and draw upon years of industry experience to not only develop your product but support it with market studies, product branding development, material expertise, merchandising and the many other intricacies that come with bringing exceptional product lines to market. Our agile development capabilities are grounded in our ability to prototype in-house as well as in cooperation with overseas production. We value our clients and enjoy many long term client relationships to whom we have delivered complete packages starting with a blank page and ending with award-winning products. Those projects have involved sourcing and production both in the USA and internationally and have spanned from traditional to novel products. Throughout these relationships, we remain flexible and efficient while providing a quality collaborative relationship with our clients. We transmute basic needs into successful products and exceptional value through a holistic and systematic design process.
