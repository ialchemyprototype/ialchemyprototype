La Sportiva

Hi5 skis

Ultralight and made for backcountry powder
La Sportiva partnered with us in designing their first-ever ski. That ski, the Hi5, evolved into an entire ski line in which we were involved for many years, bringing subsequent products and improvements to life. The skis went on to receive rave reviews. In subsequent seasons, we worked on the Hang 5 and then the Vapor Nano ski, an ultralight evolution of the Hi5, that used innovative materials to achieve an incredibly low weight.
